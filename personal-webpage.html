Hi I'm Sam

My Online Name

I'm Sam, but most people online call me fish. I adopted this name a long time ago and it has stuck with me. The name was created on the original Xbox in 2002 for my brother who was an avid fisher and I was only one year old at the time. My dad created a gamertag for his new Xbox Live profile and the name he came up with was FishboyX24X7X. The name is clearly dated with the use of X's. We eventually started sharing the account and over time I eventually took ownership of the account. Throughout the years the name has been shortened going from FishboyX24X7X to Fishboy and now to fish.

Along with my name I have also had an interesting story to go with my profile picture that begins with a Mcdonald's Happy Meal. Back in 2005 the Disney movie Chicken Little was releasing and had a collaboration with McDonald's in which the characters would come included with a Happy Meal. Somehow the Fish out of Water character from the movie came into my father's possession. At the time he was a teacher and would decorate his room with different items and decided he wanted to add it to the room. Years later when he was being moved to a new classroom my family and I went to help pack up the room and I stumbled across the character and thought it would make a fitting profile picture for my Xbox account. Since then I have used the name and picture for any non professional accounts I own. I don't forsee myself coming up with a new or better online persona anytime soon so for now it will be a part of me!
